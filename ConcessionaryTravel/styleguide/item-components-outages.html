2.2 Service Outages

Standard message area to contain messages of scheduled service disruption. This is included in Firmstep subform CT Planned Service Interruption. Visibility of this subform is controlled using root-level token {servicemessage}

Example

Planned Services Interruption

Monday 14th January, 2019

From mid-day (12:00am) until 1:30pm

The Firmstep Concessionary Travel processes will be unavailable for use during the above times due to system upgrades. Please ensure you complete any work in Firmstep prior to the start time – otherwise your work may not be saved correctly.

We will circulate everyone when the works have been completed and the processes become available again for you to use.
