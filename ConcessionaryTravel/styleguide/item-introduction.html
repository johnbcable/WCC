2 Introduction

Firmstep components are styled ingredients of our CT application. They may be made up of smaller groups of styles. The styling of these components is specified in this (CT.css) file which is loaded into CT Firmstep processes using the static link: http://static.warwickshire.gov.uk//firmstep/processes/concessionary_travel/css/CT.css as used in the WCC CT Initialise subform.
