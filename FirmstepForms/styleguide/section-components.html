2 Components

These are styles applied to Firmstep components of our CT application. They may be made up of smaller groups of styles. The styling of these components is specified in this (CT.css) file which is loaded into CT Firmstep processes using the static link: http://static.warwickshire.gov.uk//firmstep/processes/concessionary_travel/css/CT.css as used in the WCC CT Initialise subform.

2.2 Service Outages

Standard message area to contain messages of scheduled service disruption. This is included in Firmstep subform CT Planned Service Interruption. Visibility of this subform is controlled using root-level token {servicemessage}

Example

Planned Services Interruption

Monday 14th January, 2019

From mid-day (12:00am) until 1:30pm

The Firmstep Concessionary Travel processes will be unavailable for use during the above times due to system upgrades. Please ensure you complete any work in Firmstep prior to the start time – otherwise your work may not be saved correctly.

We will circulate everyone when the works have been completed and the processes become available again for you to use. 

<div class="serviceDisruptionContainer">
<h3 class="serviceDisruptionTitle">Planned Services Interruption</h3>
<h5 class="serviceDisruptionSubTitle">Monday 14<sup>th</sup> January, 2019</h5>
<p class="serviceDisruptionPeriod">
	From mid-day (12:00am) until 1:30pm
</p>
<p>
The <strong>Firmstep Concessionary Travel</strong> processes will be unavailable for use during the above times due to system upgrades. Please ensure you complete any work in Firmstep prior to the start time – otherwise your work may not be saved correctly.
</p>
<p>
We will circulate everyone when the works have been completed and the processes become available again for you to use.
</p>
</div>

2.4 Developer Notes

Defines the way in which notes for Firmstep developers appear. These are normally implemented as HTML tokens with display condition 1=2 located physically adjacent in the Firmstep source to the Firmstep controls it relates to. so that they appear in the developer window but not at run-time

Example

Developer notes. These may optionally also contain .noteTitle elements

A Note Title

<div class="developerNotes">Developer notes. These may optionally
also contain .noteTitle elements
<p class="noteTitle">A Note Title</p></div>
