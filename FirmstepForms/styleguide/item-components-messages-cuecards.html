2.5.1 Cue Cards

Styles for displaying messages to the operator that they need to read out to the customer.

Example

Please read the following text out to the customer:

When we take payment for your Concessionary Travel pass you will not be able to cancel this

<div class="cue-card">
    <p>Please read the following text out to the customer:</p>
    <p class="prompt">When we take payment for your Concessionary Travel pass you will not be able to cancel this</p>
</div>
